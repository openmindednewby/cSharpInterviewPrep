# Quick Reference Resources

Use these links for targeted refreshers during the prep sprint. Prioritize the sections aligned with your weakest areas.

## .NET & C# Fundamentals
- [Official C# Documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/dotnet/csharp/) – Language reference and tutorials.
- [Async/Await Best Practices](https://learn.microsoft.com/dotnet/csharp/programming-guide/concepts/async/) – Deep dive on asynchronous programming.
- [Performance Guidelines for .NET](https://learn.microsoft.com/dotnet/standard/performance/) – Memory, GC, and allocation tips.

## Architecture & Patterns
- [ASP.NET Core Fundamentals](https://learn.microsoft.com/aspnet/core/fundamentals/?view=aspnetcore-8.0) – Middleware, DI, configuration.
- [Polly Resilience Library](https://www.thepollyproject.org/) – Retry, circuit breaker, and bulkhead patterns.
- [Refactoring.Guru Design Patterns](https://refactoring.guru/design-patterns) – Illustrated explanations and examples.

## Messaging & Distributed Systems
- [RabbitMQ Tutorials](https://www.rabbitmq.com/getstarted.html) – Official guide for queues, exchanges, and routing.
- [ZeroMQ Guide](https://zguide.zeromq.org/docs/chapter1/) – Concepts and socket patterns.
- [Outbox Pattern Explained](https://microservices.io/patterns/data/transactional-outbox.html) – Reliable event publishing.

## Data Layer
- [SQL Server Query Performance Tuning](https://learn.microsoft.com/sql/relational-databases/performance/performance-tuning?view=sql-server-ver16) – Indexing and execution plans.
- [PostgreSQL Window Functions](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/current/tutorial-window.html) – Rolling calculations.
- [Redis Patterns](https://redis.io/docs/latest/develop/use-cases/) – Caching strategies and data structures.

## Trading & Domain Context
- [MT5 Gateway API Overview](https://www.metaquotes.net/en/metatrader5/api) – Official documentation.
- [FIX Protocol Introduction](https://www.fixtrading.org/what-is-fix/) – Useful for trade messaging context.
- [Forex Market Basics](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/f/forex.asp) – Quick refresher on instruments and terminology.

## Behavioral & Communication
- [STAR Interview Technique](https://www.mindtools.com/a3mi01n/star-technique) – Structure for storytelling.
- [Psychological Safety Under Pressure](https://hbr.org/2022/08/how-to-stay-calm-when-you-know-youll-be-stressed) – Staying composed during tough conversations.
