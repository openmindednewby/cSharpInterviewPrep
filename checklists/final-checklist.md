# Final Interview Checklist (15 Minutes Before)

Use this quick rundown to ensure you are mentally, technically, and logistically ready when the call starts.

## 1. Environment & Logistics
- [ ] Quiet space secured, notifications silenced, background tidy.
- [ ] Headset/mic tested, video framing checked, backup audio plan ready.
- [ ] Stable internet confirmed; hotspot or tether backup prepared.
- [ ] Resume, portfolio links, and relevant code samples open on standby.

## 2. Mindset & Energy
- [ ] Take 5 deep breaths; slow exhale to settle nerves.
- [ ] Recall a recent win to boost confidence.
- [ ] Commit to active listening and concise answers.

## 3. Technical Highlights
- [ ] Ready to explain key architecture story (service you designed/refactored).
- [ ] Have one performance optimization example and one debugging example.
- [ ] Prepared to outline price streaming/order execution system design at a high level.

## 4. Domain Context
- [ ] Short explanation of MT4/MT5 integration approach rehearsed.
- [ ] Quick summary of risk controls (margin checks, throttling, auditing).
- [ ] Understand how your past work ties to trading systems or high-throughput services.

## 5. Behavioral Stories
- [ ] STAR notes for leadership/ownership scenario.
- [ ] STAR notes for collaboration under pressure.
- [ ] Example of process improvement or automation effort.

## 6. Questions for HFM
- [ ] Two thoughtful questions ready (team collaboration, technical roadmap, growth).
- [ ] Confirm you know the interviewer names/roles if provided.

## 7. Final Touches
- [ ] Hydrate and keep water nearby.
- [ ] Close unrelated apps/browser tabs.
- [ ] Join the call 3-5 minutes early.

> ✅ You’re set. Focus on clarity, confidence, and genuine curiosity.
